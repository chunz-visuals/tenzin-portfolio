Student Study Helper

Helping university students study smarter using AI tools.

About This Website

This website introduces useful AI tools and simple study methods that help students learn more efficiently and save time.

Useful AI Tools

  • ChatGPT – Ask questions and understand difficult topics.
  • Grammarly – Improve English writing.
  • DeepL – Translate documents and articles.
  • Canva AI – Create presentations quickly.

Study Tips

  1. Study a little every day.
  2. Review notes before exams.
  3. Take short breaks every hour.
  4. Use AI to explain difficult concepts.
  5. Practice with quizzes.

Benefits

Students can improve productivity, understand difficult subjects faster, and organize their learning with AI-powered tools.
